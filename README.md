**IPO Market Trends Dashboard (2010 - 2025 Jun)**

**Interactive Excel dashboard** 
Analyzing IPO market trends (2010–June 2025) — includes KPIs, listing gains, subscription levels and retail vs institutional participation.

![IPO Market](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/69a26387-26fe-49e3-96ef-bded861069ef)

Overview
This repository contains an Excel dashboard built with Power Query, Power Pivot, Pivot Tables and charts to analyze IPO performance and subscription trends in India from 2010 to June 2025.

1. Main KPIs:
  - Total IPOs: 559  
  - Total Issue Size: ₹786,192.50 Crores  
  - Average Listing Gain: 18.08%  
  - Max Listing Gain: 252.76%  
  - Retail Participation: 42.51%

Objective
To provide a concise, visual analysis of IPO trends to help investors and analysts identify patterns in listing gains, subscription behavior, and the role of retail vs institutional investors.

2. Insight:
  -IPO fundraising peaked during 2021 and 2023, showing strong market sentiment and liquidity inflows post-pandemic.
  -Sigachi Industries and Paras Defence were among top performers, offering over 200% listing gains, reflecting investor enthusiasm for niche or tech-driven IPOs.
  -Latent View and Paras Defence IPOs witnessed record-breaking subscription levels above 300x, indicating strong retail frenzy.
  -Smaller issue-size IPOs generally delivered higher percentage listing gains, showing higher risk–reward potential in smaller offerings.
  -Institutional (QIB) participation shows a steady rise, while retail participation surged post-2020, reflecting broader market democratization.
  -Nearly 70% of IPOs listed at a premium, confirming that the IPO market has generally rewarded investors over the studied period.

3. Tools & Technologies:  
  - **Microsoft Excel**
  - **Power Query** (Data Cleaning & ETL)
  - **Power Pivot (DAX)** for KPIs
  - **Pivot Tables & Charts**
  - **Slicers for interactivity**

4. Key Insights:
  - 559 IPOs raised over ₹7.8 lakh crore between 2010–2025.  
  - IPO activity peaked in **2021 and 2023**, reflecting strong bull runs.  
  - **69% of IPOs** listed at a premium, rewarding investors.  
  - **Retail participation** averaged 42.5%, while institutional (QIB) participation grew steadily.  
  - Smaller IPOs tended to yield higher listing gains.  
  - **Sigachi Industries** and **Paras Defence** topped listing gain charts with >200% returns.  
  - **Latent View** and **Paras Defence** were most subscribed, exceeding 300x.
